# rsa features

### Dungeons

- **Auto Routes**
  - 0t Autoroutes
- **Puzzles**
  - Auto Ice Fill
  - Auto Tic Tac Toe
- **Blood Blink**
  - Opens blood instantly
- **Dungeon Breaker**
  - No fatigue
- **Fast Leap**
  - Left click to auto leap to configured player
- **Secrets**
  - Auto Close chests
  - Triggerbot
  - Aura
- **Hitboxes**
  - Larger hitboxes for levers, buttons and essence
- **Auto SS**
  - Automatically completes the first device
- **Align Aura**
  - Automatically completes the third device
- **Auto4**
  - Automatically completes the fourth device
- **Auto Croesus**
  - Auto opens Dungeon rewards
- **Terminal Aura**
  - Auto opens terminals
- *AutoTerms**
  - Auto complete terminals
- **InstaMid**
  - Teleports you to necron's platform if you were standing there before

### Movement

- **Velocity Buffer**
    - Allows you to buffer velocity and essentially fly

### Other

- **Auto GFS**
  - Auto get from sacks

### Player

- **Bonzo Helper**
    - Makes bonzo staff more consistent
- **Cancel Interact**
    - Cancels interactions on certain blocks

### Render

- **Freecam**
    - Allows you to freely fly around in loaded chunks, without actually moving the player
- **ESP**
    - Mob ESP for dungeons
- **Hide**
    - Adds an option to hit though playes to the RSM Hide module
